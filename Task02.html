    Rohit Sharma's Test captaincy its first huge test

    Playing as a captain he has played a total of 16 matches where he has won a total of 10 matches with win percentage of 62.5% and have lost 4 matches against Australia twice and South Africa and England for once
    He overtook Dhoni as the most successful T20I captain with 49 wins in 62 matches. Dhoni had 41 victories from 72 games.
    Shastri places the two on equal footing, calling Rohit 'one of the best captains ever alongside Dhoni' with both showing great tactical acumen in white-ball cricket.
    Rohit Sharma is an acclaimed Indian cricketer, known for his exceptional batting skills and his ability to consistently score large runs in international cricket.
    Born on April 30, 1987, in Nagpur, Maharashtra, Sharma has risen to become one of India's most accomplished cricketers.
